Advanced Game Guide

Tile Mechanics

  • Blue Tiles: Your territory - generates troops
  • Red Tiles: Bot territory - attacks automatically
  • Gray Tiles: Neutral - has defense troops
  • Number: Current troop count on tile
  • Shield Icon: Difficulty-based defense bonus

NEW Combat System

  • Difference-Based: 21 vs 20 = 21 wins with 1 left
  • Shield System: Each tile has difficulty-based defense
  • Custom Deployment: Use bottom slider to choose troops
  • No Auto-Takeover: Only targeted tile is affected
  • Max 50 troops per tile

Strategy Tips

  • Expand early to generate more troops
  • Consolidate troops before attacking
  • Watch for bot attacks on your borders
  • Use bottom slider for precise troop deployment
  • Consider shield values when attacking

Scoring

  • +50 points per conquered tile
  • +100 points per bot defeated
  • Time bonus: +2 points per second
  • Troop bonus: +0.5 points per troop
  • Difficulty multiplier affects all scores
