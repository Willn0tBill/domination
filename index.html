How to Play Domination

Game Modes

  • Domination: The official mode! Conquer tiles while bots spawn over time. Your score is saved to the leaderboard.
  • Bots Battle: Fight multiple bots simultaneously. Last one standing wins!
  • 1v1 Challenge: Face an elite bot in intense single combat.

Basic Rules

  • Each turn, you can conquer adjacent neutral tiles (gray)
  • To attack a bot tile (red), first select one of your tiles (it will glow gold), then click an adjacent bot tile
  • Bot strength increases over time
  • In Domination mode, more bots spawn as the game progresses

Scoring

  • +10 points for each conquered tile
  • +50 points for defeating a bot
  • +5 points per turn survived (bonus at game end)
  • Difficulty multiplier affects all scores

Tips & Strategies

  • Expand strategically to create defensive barriers
  • Attack weaker bot tiles first
  • In Domination mode, focus on rapid early expansion
  • Watch the game log for important events
