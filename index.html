How to Play Domination

Master the battlefield with these essential strategies

Basic Controls

  • Select Tile: Click on any tile you control
  • Move/Attack: Select your tile, then click adjacent tile
  • Troop Slider: Use bottom slider to choose deployment amount
  • Zoom: Use +/- buttons (Domination mode only)

Combat System

  • Difference-Based: Attacker needs more troops than defender
  • Shield System: Tiles have defense bonuses
  • Custom Deployment: Choose exact troop amount
  • Max Troops: 100 troops per tile

Tile Types

  • Blue: Your territory
  • Red: Enemy territory
  • Gray: Neutral land
  • Gold Border: Selected tile

Game Modes

Domination: Infinite survival, expanding map 25x25 every 5 minutes

Bots Battle: Eliminate all AI opponents in fixed arena

1v1 Duel: Defeat the elite AI commander
