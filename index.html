Domination Tactical Guide

Master the battlefield with these essential strategies

Basic Controls

  • Select Tile: Click on any tile you control
  • Move Troops: Select your tile, then click adjacent friendly tile
  • Attack: Select your tile, then click adjacent enemy/neutral tile
  • Deselect: Click anywhere empty or press ESC

Combat Mechanics

  • You need 1.2x more troops than defender to conquer
  • Defenders get a 10% combat bonus
  • Attacking uses 50% of selected tile's troops
  • Troops regenerate on all controlled tiles over time
  • Maximum 50 troops per tile

Tile Types

  • Blue: Your territory - generates troops
  • Red: Enemy territory - attacks automatically
  • Gray: Neutral land - has defense troops
  • Gold Border: Selected tile ready for action

Advanced Strategy

  • Expand early to increase troop generation
  • Create buffer zones with neutral tiles
  • Consolidate troops before major attacks
  • Watch for bot troop movements on borders
  • In Domination mode, survival is key to high scores
