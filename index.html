How to Play

Basic Controls:

1. Click your tile to select it

2. Click adjacent enemy tile to attack

3. Click adjacent friendly tile to move troops

4. Use slider to choose troop deployment %

Game Modes:

Domination: Map grows 25x25 every 5 minutes

Bots Battle: Eliminate all AI opponents

1v1 Duel: Defeat the elite AI
